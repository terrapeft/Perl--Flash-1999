Brief Facts

Birth date: 03/03/19XX  :)

Julie Bowen raised in Baltimore, Md.
She was trained at The Actors' Institute, Will Geer's Theatricum and Shakespeare & Co. The actress attended Brown University, where she starred in Guys and Dolls, Stage Door, Lemon Sky and other productions. She speaks fluent Italian and is an avid sportswoman who especially likes skiing, swimming and skydiving.

More Details

Bet you didn't think anyone who majored in Italian Renaissance studies at Brown University could end up starring on a mystery-spy-thriller TV show, replete with guns, gadgets and high heels. That's Julie Bowen for you, a bundle of contradictions who plays Amanda Webb on the WB's new "Mod Squad"-esque "Three." When asked about "Three," Bowen describes: "It is about me wearing short shirts carrying big guns... oh, and there are two other guys."

One thing people don't usually know about me is: 

I read books more than I go out. As a matter of fact, I get a little concerned about some of my anti-social habits. I will choose a night with Somerset Maugham or Russell Banks over a crowded bar any day. 

What is the best showbiz advice you ever got? 

It's a war of attrition. If you have patience and a modicum of faith in yourself your chances are not too bad.
